Travel Information

Flights to Krabi (KBV)

Option 1: Manila - Singapore - Krabi

  • Estimated Price: 10,000 - 15,000 PHP
  • Airlines: Scoot, Singapore Airlines + local carrier, etc.

Option 2: Manila - Bangkok (Direct) then Local Flight: Bangkok - Krabi

  • Manila - Bangkok (BKK/DMK): 5,000 - 10,000 PHP (Cebu Pacific, PAL, AirAsia)
  • Bangkok - Krabi (KBV): 3,000 - 5,000 PHP (Thai Vietjet, AirAsia, Bangkok Airways)

Tip: Book in advance for better prices! Check Skyscanner, Google Flights, or individual airline websites.

Accommodation

Important Note for Entourage: Tinidee Hotel will be our main base. We will coordinate your bookings separately.

Getting to Tonsai/Railey: Longtail boats are the primary mode of transport from Ao Nang or Krabi Town to Tonsai and Railey beaches. Be prepared – you might get a little wet during boarding/alighting as boats pull up close to the shore!

Tonsai Beach (Wedding Location)

Budget-friendly bungalows to mid-range resorts. Known for its laid-back, rock-climbing vibe.

  • Hotel Option 1 (e.g., Tonsai Bay Resort - example)
  • Hotel Option 2 (e.g., Dream Valley Resort - example)
  • Hotel Option 3 (e.g., Garden View Resort - example)

Railey Beach (5-min boat ride from Tonsai)

Beautiful beaches, more resort options than Tonsai. Divided into Railey West (pricier, sunset views) and Railey East (more budget/mid-range, access to amenities).

  • Hotel Option (e.g., Railey Village Resort - example)
  • Hotel Option (e.g., Bhu Nga Thani Resort - example)
  • Hotel Option (e.g., Sand Sea Resort - example)

Ao Nang (Mainland, ~20-min boat ride to Tonsai)

Wider range of hotels, restaurants, and shops. Good base if you plan to do more tours or prefer more amenities.

  • Hotel Option 1 (e.g., Holiday Ao Nang Beach Resort - example)
  • Hotel Option 2 (e.g., Ava Sea Resort - example)
  • Hotel Option 3 (e.g., Tan Hostel - for younger crowd / budget)
