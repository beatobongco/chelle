Beaches & Local Area

The three main beaches in the area: Ao Nang, Railay (East & West), and Ton Sai

Ton Sai Beach is nestled between Railay West and Ao Nang. It's a tranquil cove with limestone cliffs, accessible only by boat. All wedding festivities will be centered here to avoid transportation hassles.
