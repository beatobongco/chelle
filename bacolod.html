FAQs

Can I bring a plus one?
Due to limited capacity, we are only able to accommodate the guests listed on your invitation. Thank you for understanding.
Are kids allowed?
Yes, children are welcome to join the celebration if they are part of the guest list. We can't wait to see them and hope they have a wonderful time.
What time should I arrive?
The ceremony will begin at 2PM and the reception will start at 5PM. We suggest to please arrive on time.
Will there be transportation?
For our visiting guests, YES. There will be a dedicated van that will service all your transportation needs. From airport to hotel to the venues. More details will be shared closer to the date.
What is the RSVP deadline?
Kindly RSVP by September 1, 2025. You can RSVP through our wedding website or by contacting us directly.
Where to stay?
We highly recommend staying in Citadines Hotel because of its proximity to the church. Other options: L'Fisher, Stone Hill, The Henry & Seda. You can also check nearby AirBnBs.
What is the dress code?
For the ladies, a long dress or Filipiniana dress. For the gents, Barong Tagalog or a long sleeve polo. (No white, black and shades of pink)
What dates should we book?
We suggest to arrive on November 7 (Friday) and stay until November 9 (Sunday).
